About

Why this space exists

My name is Jonathan. My mom has dementia. I lost my dad years ago, and there's still a place inside me that hasn't quite healed from it. I know I'm not the only one.

When my dad died, I remember how quickly the world expected me to get back to normal. And when I started watching my mom slowly become someone I barely recognized, I realized there aren't many places to sit with that kind of slow grief — the kind that doesn't have a funeral at the end of it.

Held Space is what I wish had existed for me. It's a quiet space to talk, to be heard, and to feel less alone. Whether you're caring for a parent losing themselves to dementia, still torn up about someone you lost years ago, or trying to make meaning out of experiences you can't quite put into words — you're welcome here.
